OUR STORY

JR Interior

WHO WE ARE

A Legacy of Craftsmanship & Vision

Founded in 2008 by celebrated designer Arjun Rathore, Aurum Interiors was born from a singular vision: to create living spaces that are not just beautiful, but profoundly meaningful.

Over 18 years, we have evolved from a boutique design practice into one of India's most respected luxury interior studios, with projects spanning Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, and internationally in Dubai and London.

Every space we design carries our signature philosophy — that true luxury lies in the harmony of materials, light, proportion, and personal story. We don't just design rooms; we craft experiences.
