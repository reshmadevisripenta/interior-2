QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked

How long does a typical interior design project take?

Project timelines vary by scope. A standard apartment typically takes 3–5 months, while larger villas or commercial projects can range from 6–18 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.

Do you take on projects outside of Mumbai?

Absolutely. We have completed projects across India — Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Goa — and internationally in Dubai and London. We manage remote projects with regular site visits and a trusted network of local contractors.

Can I see a 3D visualization before committing to a design?

Yes. Our Signature and Prestige packages include photorealistic 3D renders and virtual walkthroughs. We want you to feel completely confident in your design before any construction begins.

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes. We offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation — in-studio, at your site, or via video call — to understand your project and explore how we can help. No obligation, no pressure.
